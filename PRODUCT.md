# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers and engineering leads using self-hosted GitLab CE/EE. They use GLabTouch on iPhone while away from a desk to review merge requests, approve or revoke approvals, inspect diffs, and check CI pipeline health.

## Product Purpose

GLabTouch is an open-source iOS app for mobile merge request approval and pipeline monitoring on self-hosted GitLab instances. Success means a reviewer can understand the review state, pipeline state, and next safe action quickly on a small screen.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, native. The interface should feel like a trustworthy iOS tool for engineering work: fast to scan, conservative with emphasis, and clear about destructive actions.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page styling, decorative motion, full-feature GitLab client sprawl, playful status treatments, and custom controls that fight native iOS expectations.

## Design Principles

- Optimize for one-handed review triage and quick confidence.
- Keep GitLab state legible through native iOS hierarchy, spacing, and semantic color.
- Make risky actions explicit and reversible where GitLab allows.
- Use motion to clarify state changes, especially reveal, collapse, loading, and completion.
- Preserve the zero third-party dependency direction by using SwiftUI-native patterns.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect Dynamic Type, VoiceOver labels, semantic colors, and reduced motion preferences. Keep status meaning available through text and iconography rather than color alone.
